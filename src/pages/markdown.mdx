<details class="theorem" open>
<summary>The Three Isomorphism theorems</summary>

1. If $\varphi:G\to G'$ is a group homomorphism, then
  $$
  G/\ker\varphi\cong \varphi(G)
  $$
2. If $H\le G$ and $N\triangleleft G$, then
  $$
  H/(H\cap N)\cong HN/N
  $$
3. If $K,N\triangleleft G$ and $K\le N$, then
  $$
  (G/K)/(N/K)\cong G/N
  $$
</details>

<details class="example list-none" open>
<summary>Show that any normal subgroup is the kernel of some homomorphism</summary>
**Proof.** Let any $N\triangleleft G$, define $\varphi:G\to G/N$ by $\varphi(g)=gN$ for any $g\in G$

WTS $\varphi$ is homomorphism
- Let any $a,b\in G$, wts $\varphi(ab)=\varphi(a)\varphi(b)$
$$
\begin{aligned}
\because&&(aN)(bN)&=(ab)N\\
\therefore&&\varphi(a)\varphi(b)&=(aN)(bN)=(ab)N=\varphi(ab)
\end{aligned}
$$
- $\therefore\varphi$ is homomorphism as wanted.

WTS $N=\ker\varphi$, consider any $g\in G$

$$
\begin{aligned}
  \because&& g\in\ker\varphi&\iff N=\varphi(g)=gN\iff g\in N\\
  \therefore&&\ker\varphi&=N
\end{aligned}
$$

$\therefore N=\ker\varphi$, that is, the kernel of some homomorphism $\quad\square$

</details>

<details class="algorithm" open>
<summary>Show Language $L$ is in $NP$</summary>
1. Show $L$ is a decision problem.
2. For any $w\in L$, provide a certificate $c$ of $w$.
3. Show $c$ is polynomial in size with respect to $|w|$.
4. Provide a verifier $V$ that decides $\langle w,c\rangle$.
5. Show $V$ runs in polynomial time.
</details>
